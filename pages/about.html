Our Story

From a street cart to your door

"Ăn là nhớ" means eat it and you'll remember. We started this because the bánh tráng trộn we grew up with — the real Tây Ninh kind — was nowhere to be found in the US. So we brought it here ourselves.

The name

What "Ăn Là Nhớ" means

In Vietnamese, ăn là nhớ means "eat it and you'll remember." It's not a marketing line — it's the most honest review bánh tráng trộn has ever received. One bite takes you back: to a school gate in Tây Ninh, to your grandmother's kitchen, to a street corner you haven't visited in years. The snack carries memory in every ingredient.

The origin

Chính gốc Tây Ninh

Tây Ninh province, tucked along the Cambodian border northwest of Hồ Chí Minh City, is the undisputed home of bánh tráng trộn. The province has been producing bánh tráng and muối tôm for generations — the local rice paper is thicker and smokier than anything made elsewhere, and the shrimp salt, made from fragrant Vàm Cỏ river shrimp, has a depth of flavor that can't be replicated.

When people in other cities say their bánh tráng trộn is "Tây Ninh style," they mean it's the best style. We don't imitate it — we source from there directly.

Our mission

No shortcuts. No substitutes.

A lot of bánh tráng trộn sold in the US is a compromise — generic rice paper, imitation shrimp salt, simplified recipes. It's fine. But it's not what we grew up eating.

Ăn Là Nhớ exists to close that gap. We work directly with Tây Ninh producers to source authentic bánh tráng and muối tôm. We mix in small batches by hand, seal each bag immediately for freshness, and ship across the US within 1–2 business days. Nothing is compromised to make the logistics easier.

What we stand for

Three things we never compromise on

🌶️

Authenticity

We source every ingredient from Tây Ninh or from suppliers who work the same way Tây Ninh producers do. If we can't get the real thing, we don't make it.

👐

Small-batch quality

Every bag is mixed by hand in small batches. There's no machine that can distribute the ingredients the way a person can — each piece of rice paper deserves attention.

🤝

Community

We exist to serve Vietnamese communities in the US who miss the taste of home — and to introduce one of Vietnam's greatest snacks to anyone who hasn't tried it yet.

Who we are

TN Delicacy Distribution LLC

We're a Vietnamese-American family business based in the United States, with deep roots in Tây Ninh province. Our founders grew up eating bánh tráng trộn from street carts, and started Ăn Là Nhớ because the diaspora deserved better than the imitations available here.

We handle everything: sourcing, mixing, packaging, and fulfillment. We work with a small team and we know every bag that goes out the door. If you ever have a question — about an order, about the ingredients, about a wholesale partnership — reach out directly. We actually read the emails.
